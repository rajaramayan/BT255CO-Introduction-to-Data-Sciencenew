# Unit 8 — Data Science Ethics and Privacy
**Duration: 6 Hours**

---

## Overview

As Data Science becomes deeply embedded in society — in hiring systems, credit scoring, healthcare, law enforcement — the ethical dimensions become critically important. This unit examines the principles, regulations, and practical considerations for ethical and responsible Data Science.

## Quick Reference

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Data Ethics | Framework of principles for responsible data collection and use |
| GDPR | General Data Protection Regulation (EU, 2018) |
| CCPA | California Consumer Privacy Act (USA, 2020) |
| DPDP | Digital Personal Data Protection Act (India, 2023) |
| Algorithmic Bias | Systematic unfairness in ML model outputs |
| Data Anonymisation | Removing personally identifiable information |
| Consent | Informed agreement to collect and use personal data |
| Transparency | Ability of stakeholders to understand how decisions are made |
