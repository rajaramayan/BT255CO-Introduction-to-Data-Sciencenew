# Unit 4 — Descriptive Statistics
**Duration: 4 Hours**

---

## Overview

Descriptive Statistics summarise and describe the main features of a dataset. Before building models, every data scientist must **understand the data** through statistical summaries and visualisations. This unit covers measures of central tendency, measures of dispersion, and visualisation with Matplotlib and Seaborn.

## Quick Reference

| Measure | Symbol | When to Use |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Mean | $\bar{x}$ | Symmetric data, no extreme outliers |
| Median | M | Skewed data or when outliers are present |
| Mode | Mo | Categorical data; most frequent value |
| Variance | $\sigma^2$ | Spread of data around the mean |
| Std Deviation | $\sigma$ | Average distance from the mean |
| IQR | $Q_3 - Q_1$ | Spread of the middle 50%; robust to outliers |
